Is there a visible pattern between neighbourhoods reporting missing person incidents?

Answer

From this Prototype, it is clearly visible that highest number of missing person incidents are from "Tenderloin", "Mission" and "South of Market" neighbourhoods. The month reporting the highest number of incidents is a summer month(June - August) and the maximum number of incidents occur at 12:00 PM PST in morning on Monday accross these neighbourhoods.

Encoding

Number of Incidents in each neighbourhood of SF is encoded over sequential color range. Darker the color, more number of incidents are in that particular neighbourhood.

Planned Interactivity

Details on demand Interactivity and on clicking over the neighbourhood in map will show the hourly and monthly trend of that neighbourhood as shown in the prototypes above.
